# Contributing to StickCraft

Thank you for your interest in contributing to StickCraft! 🎉
We're a non-profit, community-first open-source STEAM education project, and every contribution matters.

---

## Ways to Contribute

### 🛠 Design Files
- Submit new kit designs (CAD, STL, or printable templates)
- Improve existing designs for better durability or accessibility
- Adapt designs for different age groups or material constraints

### 📖 Guides & Curriculum
- Write or improve assembly guides
- Create activity or lesson plan templates
- Translate guides into other languages

### 🐛 Bug Reports & Suggestions
- Found an issue with a design or guide? Open an issue!
- Have a feature idea? We'd love to hear it.

---

## How to Submit

1. **Fork** this repository
2. **Create a branch**: `git checkout -b feature/your-feature-name`
3. **Commit** your changes with a clear message
4. **Open a Pull Request** describing what you added or changed

---

## Guidelines

- Keep all materials **low-cost and accessible** — this is core to StickCraft's mission
- All submissions must be your own original work or properly licensed
- Be respectful and constructive in all interactions
- Label your files clearly (e.g., `bridge-kit-v2.stl`, `lesson-plan-grade5.pdf`)

---

## Code of Conduct

By contributing, you agree to treat all community members with respect and kindness.
StickCraft is a welcoming space for students, educators, and makers of all backgrounds.

---

Have questions? Open an issue or reach out through the repository. We're glad you're here! 🌱
